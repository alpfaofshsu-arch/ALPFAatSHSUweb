Who We Are

More Than a Club. A Career Launchpad.

The Association of Latino Professionals For America (ALPFA) is the nation's largest Latino professional organization. Our SHSU chapter brings together students passionate about business, accounting, finance, and technology — providing mentorship, professional development workshops, and direct access to top employers.

Whether you're a freshman figuring out your major or a senior recruiting for your first job, ALPFA at SHSU gives you the tools, the network, and the community to succeed.

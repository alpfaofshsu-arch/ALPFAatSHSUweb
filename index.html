Who We Are

More Than a Club. A Career Launchpad.

ALPFA aims to provide student members with access to a range of resources to support their success in the form of leadership opportunities, networking opportunities, workshops, and professional development.

ALPFA at SHSU is more than just a program or a resource; we are a community. We believe that by connecting students, we can create a supportive, empowering environment where everyone can thrive and develop the skills they need to become leaders and future business professionals.

We are open to all heritages and races.

ALPFA Houston

ALPFA at SHSU is proudly associated with the National Chapter, ALPFA Houston. As part of this professional network, our members gain access to a wide range of resources beyond the campus level.

ALPFA Houston provides members with valuable networking opportunities, scholarships to support academic and professional goals, and social opportunities to connect with business professionals across the Houston area and beyond.

Join ALPFA at SHSU

Join a thriving community of future business professionals. Review the requirements below and become a member today.

Membership Requirements

  • ✓Business Major: Must be enrolled as a business major at SHSU (COBA).
  • ✓GPA Requirement: Maintain a minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5.
  • ✓Annual Dues: Pay yearly membership dues of $15.
  • ✓Good Standing: Attend at least 3 professional meetings or workshops per semester to maintain active membership.

Dues Payment Information

Annual membership dues are $15. You may pay using one of the following methods:
